FAQs

What services does your diabetes clinic offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of services for people with diabetes, including diagnosis, treatment planning, education, and ongoing support.

Who is on the diabetes care team?

Our team includes endocrinologists, diabetes educators, registered nurses, dietitians, and other healthcare professionals specializing in diabetes management.

Do I need a referral to visit your clinic?

Some insurance plans may require a referral, so please check with your insurance provider beforehand. We also accept self-referrals.

What are your clinic hours?

Our clinic runs between 8am and 4pm on Mondays to Fridays. Please note that these hours may vary depending on the location.

Do you offer diabetes education classes?

Yes, we offer various educational programs on topics like healthy eating, blood sugar monitoring, medication management, and preventing complications.

Do you have support groups for diabetes patients?

We offer support groups where you can connect with others living with diabetes and share experiences, advice, and encouragement.
